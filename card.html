Scorpion

Fluorescent chemicals in the transparent layer of their shell causes them to glow under UV light.

  • Scentific Name: Scorpiones
  • Average Length: 6cm
  • Average Lifespan: 3-5 years
  • Habitat: Deserts

Scorpions have eight legs,a pair of claws, and a narrow tail with a stinger. Their average length is 6cm. They generally prey on insects. Their evolutionary history goes as far back as 435 million years.
